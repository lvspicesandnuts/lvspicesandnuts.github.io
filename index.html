    About Us

    Established in the year 1950, “Ms. Liladhar Vallabhji & Co.” Registered Firm, is promoted and managed by Tushar Bhanushali. The management has more than 45 years of business experience in Retail and Wholesale of Spices as well as Nuts. We have developed the goodwill and reputation for quality supply.


    We are specialized in providing the packing and packaging solution by supplying the goods in different quantity.


    The company has a policy of maximum flexibility in terms of both Packaging techniques and stock holding of the materials to provide a complete service to customers.
